Andee Wallace, Co-Founder of Robigo

Andee Wallace is the co-founder of Robigo, a company using synthetic biology to create eco-friendly crop treatments. Robigo develops a natural, bacteria-based pesticides found in soil and plants, which are bioengineered to fight harmful diseases without the need for toxic chemicals. Andee and Robigo's approach helps make farming more sustainable and reduces the reliance on harmful pesticides.
